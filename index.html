About Me

I am very passionate about technology and learning new technologies excites me. From the first day I placed my fingers on the keyboard of a computer, I have built a special interest in technology. I grew up in the part of the world where computers were reserved for the very rich. Even with a bachelor in business, I have always kept my interest in tech.

Straight out of college, I had the opportunity to finally realize my dream of exploring some amazing technologies. First as a NetSuite ERP project coordinator, that paved my way to exploring more interest in the tech industry. I moved into Quality Assurance and instead of keep testing what others were building, my interest was then focused on building applications myself. Coding became my next passion and it has stuck with me ever since.

I started teaching myself some basic web development skills like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. To even hold myself more accountable, I went for a full Stack certificate from the University of Minnesota web development boot camp, I had the opportunity to learn some of the current and best technologies in web development today. Among them is the MERN stack; (MongoDB, Express, React/Redux and Node js).

It has been an amazing journey so far since my first "Hello World" in terms of my skills and continued passion for development. In my current role as a Business Systems Consultant with the Enterprise Cloud Services team at Wells Fargo, I have the opportunity to work with various application teams that are migrating to the cloud, making sure that their application configurations adhere to best practices formulated by the bank. I also work with python, Java and its Spring Framework. I really enjoy working on awesome projects from games to web applications. Check out my project section for some of the many projects I have worked on. When my head is not buried in the IDE, I love traveling, cooking and spending time with family and friends. You can contact me here.
